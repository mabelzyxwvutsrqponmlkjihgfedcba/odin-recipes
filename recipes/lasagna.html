Description:

Lasagna is a classic Italian comfort dish made with layers of tender pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese. This hearty baked dish is perfect for family dinners, offering a satisfying blend of savory flavors and gooey textures. Whether you prefer a traditional beef version or a vegetarian twist with spinach and mushrooms, lasagna is a crowd-pleaser that tastes even better the next day!

Ingredients:

  • the Meat Sauce:
    • 1 lb (450g) ground beef (or Italian sausage)
    • 1 onion, finely chopped
    • 3 garlic cloves, minced
    • 1 (28oz) can crushed tomatoes
    • 2 tbsp tomato paste
    • 1 tsp dried oregano
    • 1 tsp dried basil
    • Salt & pepper to taste
    • 1/4 cup red wine (optional)
  • For the Cheese Layers:
    • 15 oz (425g) ricotta cheese
    • 1 egg (helps bind the ricotta)
    • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
    • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
    • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped

Steps:

  1. Meat Sauce:
    • 1 lb (450g) ground beef
    • 1 onion, diced
    • 3 garlic cloves, minced
    • 28oz (800g) crushed tomatoes
    • 2 tbsp tomato paste
    • 1 tsp each: dried oregano, basil, salt
    • ¼ tsp black pepper
  2. Cheese Layers
    • 15oz (425g) ricotta cheese
    • 1 egg
    • ½ cup (50g) grated Parmesan
    • 2 cups (200g) shredded mozzarella
    • 2 tbsp chopped parsley
  3. Other:
    • 9 lasagna noodles (no-boil or pre-cooked)
    • 1 tbsp olive oil
