Chocolate Cake

Home

Description

This decadent chocolate fudge cake is the ultimate dessert for chocolate lovers. With its moist, rich crumb and intense cocoa flavor, it's perfect for birthdays or special occasions. The silky chocolate ganache frosting takes it to another level of indulgence.

Ingredients:

  • Cake:
    • 1½ cups (190g) all-purpose flour
    • 1 cup (200g) granulated sugar
    • ½ cup (50g) unsweetened cocoa powder
    • 1 tsp baking soda
    • ½ tsp salt
    • 1 cup (240ml) water (or coffee for richer flavor)
    • ⅓ cup (80ml) vegetable oil
    • 1 tbsp white vinegar (or lemon juice)
    • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • Frosting Optional
    • ½ cup (115g) butter
    • ⅓ cup (30g) cocoa powder
    • 2 cups (250g) powdered sugar
    • 2–3 tbsp milk

Steps:

  1. Batter
    • Whisk 1½ cups flour, 1 cup sugar, ½ cup cocoa, 1 tsp baking soda, ½ tsp salt.
    • Add 1 cup water, ⅓ cup oil, 1 tbsp vinegar, 2 tsp vanilla. Mix until smooth.
  2. Bake
    • Pour into greased 9" pan. Bake 30-35 mins @350°F. Cool.
  3. Frosting
    • Melt ½ cup butter + ⅓ cup cocoa. Mix in 2 cups powdered sugar + 2 tbsp milk. Spread.
