Scrambled Egg

Home

Description:

Lasagna is a classic Italian comfort dish made with layers of tender pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese. This hearty baked dish is perfect for family dinners, offering a satisfying blend of savory flavors and gooey textures. Whether you prefer a traditional beef version or a vegetarian twist with spinach and mushrooms, lasagna is a crowd-pleaser that tastes even better the next day!

Ingredients:

  • Core Ingredients:
    • 4 large eggs (fresh, room temperature)
    • 1 tbsp butter (or 2 tbsp for extra richness)
    • 1 tbsp heavy cream or whole milk (optional, for creaminess)
    • Salt (¼ tsp, or to taste)
    • Black pepper (freshly cracked, to finish)
  • Optional Evaluation:
    • 1 tbsp chopped chives or fresh herbs
    • 1 tbsp crème fraîche (fold in at the end)
    • ¼ cup grated Parmesan or sharp cheddar
    • Dash of hot sauce or smoked paprika

Steps:

  1. Whisk 3 eggs + 1 tbsp cream/milk + pinch of salt until fully smooth (no streaks).
  2. Melt 1 tbsp butter in nonstick pan over medium-low heat (foamy but not brown).
  3. Pour in eggs. Let sit 10 sec, then fold gently with spatula—no stirring!
  4. Remove from heat when slightly underdone (they’ll finish cooking on residual heat).
  5. Finish with cracked pepper and chives.
